LIFETEEN

What is LIFETEEN?

Life Teen is a Catholic youth ministry organization for high school teens led by the Holy Spirit to take teens where they are right now and lead them to Christ through the Eucharist. The goal of Life Teen is to provide a safe and fun place for youth to find a solid Catholic community, get answers to their questions, and, most importantly, to experience Jesus in a profound and personal way.

Our Leadership

Fr. Dan McBride V.F.

Pastor

Fr. Dan McBride was ordained a priest in 2001 and is currently the pastor of St. Mary's Parish in Chandler, Arizona, where he has faithfully served the community. A priest of the Diocese of Phoenix, Fr. McBride brings a rich background in pastoral care and a deep commitment to the spiritual growth of his parishioners. Prior to his current role, he served in various diocesan ministries and has a strong presence in both parish life and diocesan events. Fr. McBride has a passion for serving the youth and works to inspire the next generation of Catholics. His enthusiasm for ministry, along with his love for media and audio-visual work, helps foster a welcoming and vibrant church environment for all.

Anthony Scroggins

Director of Youth Ministry

Anthony Scroggins serves as the Youth Minister at St. Mary's Parish in Chandler, Arizona, with over 11 years of experience in youth ministry. He has worked with youth of all ages, including serving with Totus Tuus in Kansas, where he led parish-based catechetical programs. He spent three years in seminary—one at Nazareth House and two at St. John Vianney Theological Seminary in Denver—studying philosophy and deepening his love for the Church. Prior to his current role, he helped launch a youth group at St. Daniel the Prophet Parish in Scottsdale. His dedication and creativity continue to inspire the youth at St. Mary's, helping them grow in faith and encounter Christ.
